How3 Score Methodology

Our scoring system analyzes protocols across multiple dimensions to provide a comprehensive quality assessment.

Earnings Quality Score (EQS)

Measures both the stability and magnitude of protocol revenue. Protocols with consistent, non-fluctuating revenue streams score higher, indicating genuine utility rather than short-term hype.

User Growth Score (UGS)

Evaluates active users, transaction count, and volume month-over-month. This score helps identify protocols with strong user adoption and engagement, even if revenue is still developing.

Fair Value Score (FVS)

Compares market capitalization to revenue to identify over/undervalued protocols. Lower market cap to revenue ratios (e.g., 10x vs 500x) indicate potentially undervalued opportunities.

Safety Score (SS)

Incorporates security audit data from Certik Skynet to assess protocol safety and security. Higher scores indicate protocols with stronger security practices and fewer vulnerabilities.
