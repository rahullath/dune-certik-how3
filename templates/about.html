About How3.io

Our Mission

Simplifying crypto investment decisions for traditional finance investors

How3.io brings traditional finance metrics to crypto protocols, helping investors understand their fundamental value without needing deep blockchain expertise. We create standardized scores across multiple dimensions to give a complete picture of a protocol's health and future potential.

Our Approach

How3.io analyzes over 50 leading protocols using data from:

  • On-chain transactional data
  • Protocol revenue figures
  • User growth metrics
  • Security assessments

Our Scoring Methodology

Earnings Quality Score (EQS)

The Earnings Quality Score measures both the stability and magnitude of a protocol's revenue streams.

Components:
  • Revenue Stability (50%): Evaluates month-over-month consistency in revenue generation
  • Revenue Magnitude (50%): Assesses revenue size relative to category peers
Why it matters

Protocols with consistent and substantial revenue streams demonstrate product-market fit and sustainable business models, making them more attractive long-term investments.

User Growth Score (UGS)

The User Growth Score evaluates adoption trends and user engagement metrics.

Components:
  • Active Addresses (40%): Number of unique addresses interacting with the protocol
  • Transaction Count (30%): Volume of transactions processed
  • Transaction Volume (30%): Total value of transactions
Why it matters

Strong user growth indicates increasing adoption and network effects, which typically translate to long-term value creation and potential revenue growth.

Fair Value Score (FVS)

The Fair Value Score compares a protocol's market capitalization to its revenue generation.

Components:
  • Price to Revenue Ratio: Compares current market cap to annual revenue
  • Category Comparison: Evaluates P/R ratio against category averages
Why it matters

This score helps identify potentially undervalued or overvalued protocols by comparing their valuations relative to their revenue generation capacity and industry peers.

Safety Score (SS)

The Safety Score evaluates technical security and vulnerability risks.

Components:
  • Code Quality: Assessment of code quality and best practices
  • Vulnerability History: Historical security incidents and responses
  • Audit Strength: Coverage and depth of security audits
Why it matters

Security is paramount in crypto investments. Protocols with strong security practices reduce the risk of hacks, exploits, and technical failures that could lead to significant losses.

How3 Score Calculation

The overall How3 Score is a weighted average of the four component scores:

How3 Score = (EQS × 25%) + (UGS × 25%) + (FVS × 25%) + (SS × 25%)

Each component is normalized to a 0-100 scale before being combined. The final How3 Score provides a comprehensive evaluation of a protocol's overall quality and investment potential.

Score Interpretation
Score Range Rating Interpretation
80-100 Excellent Top-tier protocol with strong fundamentals
70-79 Good Solid protocol with strong potential
50-69 Moderate Average protocol with some concerns
0-49 Poor Weak fundamentals, higher risk

Data Sources

How3.io leverages data from the following sources:

  • Dune Analytics: On-chain data for revenue and user metrics
  • Certik Skynet: Security audit information and vulnerability assessments
  • CoinGecko/CoinMarketCap: Market capitalization and token price data

Data is updated daily to ensure scores reflect the most current information available.
